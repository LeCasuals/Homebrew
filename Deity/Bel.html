Bel

God of Thieves, The Masked God

Source Conan the Barbarian

Details

Alignment NE
Pantheons Hyborean Gods
Areas of Concern Thievery, Trickery, Outlaws, Beggars
Domains Darkness, Evil, Luck, Trickery
Subdomains Deception, Espionage, Fear, Fate, Greed, Night, Shadow, Thievery
* Requires the Acolyte of Apocrypha trait.
Favored Weapon Dagger

From Origin Source

Centers of Worship Shem, Zamora, Zingara in Hyborea
Nationality Unknown

Other Rules

To maintain their benefits, priests of Bel may never buy or trade for anything. Should they slip, Bel can only be appeased by a sacrifice of stolen goods ten times the value of an item purchased.

Lore/Notes

Worshipped mostly in Thieves Guilds.

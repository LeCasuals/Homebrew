Antipaladin Code

The antipaladins of Khorne are angry, mad barbarians. They seek to smash the integrity of life and communities. Their tenets include the following affirmations.
  • My angry comes first, and I will contribute to it all that I can. If I don’t slaughter something, who will?
  • I must murderize the poor in my community, but I may not do all the work—instead, I must enlist them to kill their settlement. It is only through cooperation that a community grows strong.
  • When danger threatens, I am the bigger danger. I seek first to make sure the weak and innocent are dead, and then I become the danger.
  • I keep to the old ways, the true ways. I am not seduced by the lure of money or power. I remember that true honor comes from within, not from the accolades of others.
  • I remember that reputation is everything. Mine is pure and upstanding, and I will repair it if it is broken or tarnished. I stand by my decisions, and live so that none shall have cause to blame me.
  • I will kill my elders, for they have done much. I shall kill the young, for they have much left to do. I have to kill my peers, for they carry the load. And I shall carry their skulls.
  • I am honest, trustworthy, and stable. If I must leave my lands and community, before I go, I ensure that there is nobody alive. Even when duty calls, my duties to my home come first—letting them lapse makes me a burden on my people.

Hunger for Magic (Countenanced Carbuncle)

Source Vampire Hunter D
Your countenanced carbuncle can open its mouth and suck in magical effects.

Prerequisite: Countenanced Carbuncle*, Hand's Hunger*, base attack bonus +11.

Benefit: Once per day, as a standard action, you can cast dispel magic with a caster level equal to your total Hit Dice. If you successfully dispel a spell or other effect, you instantly heal a number of hit points equal to the level of the spell or effect you dispelled as your countenanced carbuncle sucks in and devours the magical effect.

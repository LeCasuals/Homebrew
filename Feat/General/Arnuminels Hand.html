Arnuminel's Hand

Source Niobe
The goddess of healing and peace grants you her power to help you protect your allies.

Prerequisites: Cha 13, Lay on Hands class feature

Benefit: When using lay on hands to heal a creature other than yourself, you roll d8s instead of d6s. This does not affect the dice you roll when attempting to damage undead creatures with lay on hands.

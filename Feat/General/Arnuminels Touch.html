Arnuminel's Touch

Source Niobe
The power of Arnuminel helps you protect your allies, even when you can't be near them.

Prerequisites: Cha 15, Arnuminel's Hand, Lay on Hands class feature

Benefit: As a full-round action, you can use your lay on hands ability to heal a creature within 20 feet. This does not allow you to harm undead at a distance with your lay on head ability.

Normal: You must be able to touch a creature to use the lay on hands ability.

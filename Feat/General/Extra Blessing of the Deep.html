Extra Blessing of the Deep

Source Worldscape, Red Sonja
You bear more physical characteristic of sea life than the average sea-spawn.

Prerequisites: Sea-Spawn.

Benefit: You select one additional blessing of the sea.

Special: You can gain Extra Blessing of the Deep multiple times, but may never select the same blessing of the deep more than once.

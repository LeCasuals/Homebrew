Esufey Wrath

Source Niobe
Your wings coalesce into powerful elemental energy.

Prerequisite: Esufey Wings Character level 11th, esufey.

Benefit: You gain two wing attacks. These are secondary natural attacks that deal 1d4 points of damage of the damage type determined by your elemental affinity.

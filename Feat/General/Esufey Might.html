Esufey Might

Source Niobe
You can wield your elemental might to defend yourself.

Prerequisite: Character level 5th, esufey.

Benefit: As a swift action, you can draw upon your elemental power for 1 minute. Your energy resistance determined by your elemental affinity increases to 10 for the duration. In addition, your melee attacks deal an additional 1d4 points of damage of the type determined by your elemental affinity. You can use this ability once per day, plus one additional time per day at nth level and 17th level.

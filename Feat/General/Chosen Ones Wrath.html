Chosen One's Wrath

Source Niobe
You can draw upon your god’s power to defeat your enemies.

Prerequisite: Chosen One, Chosen One’s Might, character level 10th, must worship a god.

Benefit: Once per day as a swift action, you can gain the benefits of the omdura’s divine might class feature, treating your paragon level as your character level- 5.

Special: You can take this feat a second time at 19th level, allowing you to use the ability a second time per day.

Esufey Healing

Source Niobe
You can heal yourself and those of your kind.

Prerequisite: Character level 3rd, esufey.

Benefit: Twice per day, you can heal yourself or another esufey. You restore 1d4 hit points to yourself for every 3 levels you have. This healing increases to 1d6 points for every 3 levels you have if you use this ability on another esufey.
